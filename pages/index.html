A vision of BACH

An Oddysey for Their Album "BACH"

  • Vámonos de viaje - Opens in a perfect way with a keyboard that reminds us of Ranma 1/2, when we have nothing to worry about.
  • Súper V - I know... she goes away, and with her, a part of us. Thanks for giving us one year of therapy.
  • El Temblor - Please don't agonize anymore. Everyone needs to stop for a moment, take a break; you aren't a robot.
  • Demasiado - How many times have you found yourself in your corner, pondering when she allowed you into her world? Perhaps it's time to discover a way out.
  • El club de la montaña - Rummaging in a failed love, pushing forward and healing little by little, it doesn't hurt anymore with this song.
  • Hermanos - Just let go, my friend. Close your eyes, lay back, and allow yourself to float along the river of soft melodies.
  • Tema de Susana - Enough of sad songs. Get up and dance with the joy that our precious "Susana" bring us.
  • Lento - Have you stopped for a moment yet? What are you waiting for? Go at your own pace, fill yourself with love; life is not a competition.
  • Tú Orbita - Never rush things, everything must be done well and in due time; less is more.
  • El concierto - All our wounds heal with a little help and willpower. It's never too late to start again.
  • Ácido - In the end, we always strive to love ourselves more, like an avalanche in the mountains, on our journey, we become stronger.

"Era el momento para estar despierto, me contaste un cuento ya no tuve miedo..."

— Bandalos Chinos, Tema de Susana.
